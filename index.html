Who's this?

Hello there! My name's Maxence Roy, also known as "Xermax" across various platforms. I'm currently a college student in Computer Science at John Abbott College, Montreal, and I wish to pursue a career in software engineering.

Ever since I was a kid, I've always loved to entertain and help the people around me. I developed a passion for creating video games at a young age, and I've been programming since I was 12 years old. My passion has led me to get actively involved in many online communities, like the Roblox community.

Through my successes and my failures, I have learned how to properly use online resources and work independently. I've also learned how to divide an ambitious assignment into efficient steps. While working on team projects, I understood the importance of communicating and making the best use of each other’s skills.

I love getting together with peers and working on awesome projects. I like to challenge myself by participating in contests and game jams. I manage the tasks I have to undertake via calendar and boards. I also love to compile and analyze data. For instance, I use Microsoft Excel to follow and visualize my daily tendencies. Outside of computer science, I enjoy skiing with friends. On top of that, I have a passion for both competitive and cooperative card / board games. From time to time, I like to edit videos to create entertaining compilations.
