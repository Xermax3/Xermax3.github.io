Projects That Inspire Me

I love to use online kanban boards, such as Trello, to organize my projects. It helps me visualize what I have to work on and helps me separate large tasks. It's especially useful when I'm working within a team. I greatly admire this aspect of software development.

As I make games on Roblox for fun, it is always inspiring and insightful for me to see my friends work on their games on the platform. It motivates me to keep working on my own projects. The picture above shows a game in development called Ventury, by glossydon (a cool dude).

Nicky Case is an independant web developer. He has made plenty of interactive simulations, which explores diverse relevant topics such as voting systems, human networks, mental health. I find his work fascinating and I would like to do projects similar to his in the future.

Undertale, by Toby Fox, is an indie RPG game released in 2015. Despite most of the game being made by a single person, it gained a huge following and became a cult classic. I find this very motivating, knowing a project made out of passion can become such a huge success.
