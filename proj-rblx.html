Roblox Projects

For those who aren't familiar, Roblox is essentially a game engine that provides servers for people to play your games for free. This platform is what inspired me to get into programming and, thanks to it, I've met a lot of great people. Although nowadays it is harder for me to find time to work on my games, my passion for the hobby remains unchanged. My priority right now is revamping my older games, which have slowly broken over time.

Feel free to check out my profile!

Note that if you want to try out my games, you will need to install Roblox on your computer and create an account.

Deep Freeze

2021, 2023?

A 3D third-person shooter rethinking of the homonymous Flash game. This was the final project for my Game Programming course in college. In this game, the player takes the role of Santa Claus, whose presents have been stolen by Halloween creatures trying to ruin Christmas. The goal is to survive the onslaught of enemies and to gather enough gifts to save the holiday. The game is technically finished. But before releasing it, I want to add more enemies and tools and make the game multiplayer.

Balloon Brawlers

2019, 2023?

Competitive game inspired by Kirby Air Ride where players gather boosts and items then fight each other to capture an exquisite plane ticket. I made this in collaboration with PleasantKillerman as part of the Adventure Club's Summer Game Jam 2019. At the moment, only the first phase of the game has been implemented. We're planning to finish the game soon though.

Quad Quests

2018-2019, 2024?

A 4-player cooperative game where everyone picks a character with a unique set of attributes and powers. Using their abilities, players collaborate to solve puzzles and collect loot. The game was created as part of my high school's personal project for the International Baccalaureate. I created game design documents and reported progress every week during meetings. I definitely want to come back to this as soon as possible!

The Legend of Max 2 Revamp

2017-2018, 2024?

The Legend of Max 2 was a game that I released in early 2014 inspired by "The Legend of Zelda" series. Over time, the game broke. This revamp aims to fix that on top of polishing and expanding every aspect of the game. Unfortunately, I never got to finish revamping the game (nearly 80% done) as other projects took priority. I will definitely come back to it one day but when that happens, I'll have to reprogram everything as the revamp code is quite old by now.

Max Adventure 3

2014

My most "well-known" and technically my latest game. Max Adventure 3 is a "collectathon" adventure game, where you explore worlds to collect items and unlock powers. This game in particular was heavily inspired by Donkey Kong 64. After a few months, 5,000 players had played the game. Some small YouTube channels even recorded themselves playing it! Automatic updates caused the game to break a few years ago, but I am currently in the process of revamping it.

The Legend of Max 2

2013-2014

A sequel to my game inspired by "The Legend of Zelda". It had a bigger world, more items, more temples; an improvement on all aspects, pretty much. Still, this was released in early 2014, and like Max Adventure 3, it broke over time. I plan to revamp it eventually.
