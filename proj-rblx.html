Roblox Projects

For those who aren't familiar with it, Roblox is a free online platform that hosts games made by its users. It is essentially a game engine that provides servers for people to play your games. Roblox is what inspired me to get into programming and because of it I've met a lot of great people. Although I haven't really released a full game since 2014, I'm still somewhat active on the platform. Feel free to check out my profile! Note that if you want to try out the games, you'll need install Roblox on your computer.

The Legend of Max 2 Revamp

2018-2020*

The Legend of Max 2 was a game I released in early 2014 inspired by "The Legend of Zelda" series. Due to updates, it broke a few years ago. Max Adventure 3

2014

My most "well-known" and latest game (released in October 2014...). Max Adventure 3 was yet another "collectathon" adventure game, where you go through a bunch of worlds to collect stuff and unlock powers. This game in particular was heavily inspired by Donkey Kong 64. I remember it being very fun to make. It gathered around 5k players after a few months, and some small YouTube channels even recorded themselves playing it. Automatic updates caused it to break around 2017, but I'm hoping to revamp it in the near future.
